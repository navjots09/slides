  Metals and Non metals 

Gold has Shine or say has Luster

Gold produces ringing sound when struck.

Gold can be converted into sheets

i.e. Gold is Malleable.

Gold has High Melting point

Gold allows heat and electricity to pass through it easily.

So it is a good conductor of electricity and heat

Sulphur has no Shine or say Lustre at all

Sulphur makes no ringing sound when struck

Sulphur cannot be converted into sheets

i.e. Sulphur is not Malleable

Sulphur has Low Melting point

Sulphur doesn’t allow heat and electricity to pass through it.
 So it is a bad conductor of electricity and heat

Elements having these properties are termed as 

Metals

Elements having these properties are termed as 

Non-Metals

Symbols of Elements

There are three general rules that we follow while deciding the symbols for the Elements.

Let's take a look at what these rules are.

Usually, First letter of the the element name is used as Symbol to represent them.

1) One Letter Symbol :

We represent them using Capital letters.
